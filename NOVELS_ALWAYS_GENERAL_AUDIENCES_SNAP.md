**Novels are always for general audiences — up to PG-13, 13+ with parental control.** The long-form story in book form — the novel — is suitable for ages 13+ with parental control; content up to PG-13. **Optional 21+ Gold Heart access** to the **novela versions** with **masculine POV** and **feminine POV** options. See [NEW_PLATFORM_NOVELS_SERIES_FEATURE_PG13_BASE_21_GOLD_HEART_UPGRADE_SNAP.md](NEW_PLATFORM_NOVELS_SERIES_FEATURE_PG13_BASE_21_GOLD_HEART_UPGRADE_SNAP.md).

## RULE

- **Novel (the book):** The story delivered as a **novel** — e.g. 3I Chief Seattle Interstellar Capture NOVEL, First Singularity, Birth Post Singularity, and all our long-form narrative books — is **always general audiences, up to PG-13, 13+ with parental control**. No 21+ requirement for reading the novel. Content standard: up to PG-13 (no R, no explicit adult content in the novel). Parents may control access or context for 13+.
- **Novela (the product):** The **novela** experience — optional 21+ Gold Heart upgrade — is **21+ and Gold Heart required**, Chairman approval. **Masculine POV** and **feminine POV** options for the novela versions. Same story; user chooses POV when booking the upgrade. See [NOVELA_21_GOLD_HEART_CHAIRMAN_APPROVAL_SNAP.md](NOVELA_21_GOLD_HEART_CHAIRMAN_APPROVAL_SNAP.md) · [NOVELA_21_PLUS_AGE_CONFIRMATION_NAUGHTY_BUTTONS_SNAP.md](NOVELA_21_PLUS_AGE_CONFIRMATION_NAUGHTY_BUTTONS_SNAP.md) · [NEW_PLATFORM_NOVELS_SERIES_FEATURE_PG13_BASE_21_GOLD_HEART_UPGRADE_SNAP.md](NEW_PLATFORM_NOVELS_SERIES_FEATURE_PG13_BASE_21_GOLD_HEART_UPGRADE_SNAP.md).

| Item | Audience |
