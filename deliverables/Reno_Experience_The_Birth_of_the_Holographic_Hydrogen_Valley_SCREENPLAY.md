## ACT ONE: THE SINGULARITY
### The Flip · The Door · The Signal

---

### FADE IN:

### EXT. DOWNTOWN RENO - VIRGINIA STREET - DAWN

The sun rises over downtown Reno. Empty shells. Faded neon. The high-water marks on the buildings. A city that looks like a tide went out and never came back.

SUPER: "DOWNTOWN RENO, NEVADA"  
SUPER: "THE SINGULARITY"  
SUPER: "AND THE REST IS HISTORY."

Camera moves slowly. Something is different today. A charge in the air. A door, about to open.

### INT. POP UP ENGINE - RESTROOM - CONTINUOUS

A man stands before the mirror. Not young, not old. Eyes that have seen the hinge of history. This is the CHAIRMAN — or the one who holds the key. He speaks to his reflection, or to us.

**CHAIRMAN**  
They say the singularity isn't one moment. It's a hinge. A door that opens once and then keeps opening. Nested shells. Higher singularities.

He grins.

**CHAIRMAN** (CONT'D)  
Recursively nested. Yeah — I know how it sounds. Nerdy. Also cool. That's the point.

He straightens his tie. Through the window: Virginia Street, the empty shells, the first light.

**CHAIRMAN** (CONT'D)  
Twain opened a door. Right here. Downtown Reno. He called it Vibelandia. The Gold Rush floodgates — they didn't just begin. They began again. And again. The rest is history. Repeating itself. To be precise.

### EXT. DOWNTOWN RENO - THE PARK - DAY

The campus. The park. People moving with purpose. Bulletin boards. Cards that are doors. Each click = full website. Novels. Episodes. Songs. Franchises. Empires. Superheroes.

SUPER: "THE BIRTH OF THE HOLOGRAPHIC HYDROGEN VALLEY"

**NARRATOR (V.O.)**  
Ground net zero ±2%. The post-singularity version of Silicon Valley. In Reno. By aware choice. The Hydrogen Holographic Valley — the new kingdom in the park. Safari skins by day. Reno neon by night. The one-two punch. What Vegas can't do. What nobody else can do.

### INT. POP UP ENGINE - MAIN ROOM - NIGHT

Neon light through the windows. The club alive. Designated Hosts. Baller V nights. Happy Endings. The other half of the punch.

**CHAIRMAN** (V.O.)  
And the rest is history. As they say. Except here it repeats. Recursively nested. In higher singularities.

**CHAIRMAN** (V.O.) (CONT'D)  
To be precise.
