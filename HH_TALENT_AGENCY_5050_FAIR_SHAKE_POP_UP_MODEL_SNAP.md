## RULE

**HH Superhero Franchise Legacy Talent Agency** serves **superhero, franchise, legacy development, operations and maintenance**. Golden · Baller V Ultimate VIP service.

- **50:50 for their billing portion — not overall.** The talent/creator's **billing portion is 50%** of revenue from their work. Fair shake always. Agency portion the other 50%. Full transparency.
- **Pop-up model.** We keep our **catalog of talent** and go with **whatever naturally fits for the pop-up mission**. Same catalog, right fit for each mission. Pop-up by nature; natural coordination. Casting and talent placement flow from the catalog to the mission that fits. **Pop-up = gig-based = win-win-win — everybody wins.** No true Viber wants a boring 9–5 job. **MJ Friendly Campus.** **BRAND T-Shirt.** See [POP_UP_GIG_BASED_WIN_WIN_WIN_MJ_FRIENDLY_CAMPUS_BRAND_TSHIRT_NEW_HIT_SNAP.md](POP_UP_GIG_BASED_WIN_WIN_WIN_MJ_FRIENDLY_CAMPUS_BRAND_TSHIRT_NEW_HIT_SNAP.md).

See [LOOKING_FOR_GOLD_HEART_ULTIMATE_V_ACTS_ARTISTS_SNAP.md](LOOKING_FOR_GOLD_HEART_ULTIMATE_V_ACTS_ARTISTS_SNAP.md).
- **Pricing.** **Basic:** billed at **$1,000 per gig per talent** (priced at $1,000 per gig). **1 · 2 · 3 · 4×4×4×4** — scaled by tier. **Baller V Ultimate VIP** = top talent tier, priced at top (e.g. **Bad Bunny** level). 50% their billing portion at every tier.
