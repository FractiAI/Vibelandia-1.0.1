## RECURSIVE AND NESTED BY DESIGN

- **Feed into:** Events, characters, and story beats in one novela **inform** the other. The room in Reno. The weekend. The big boy and the baller. The squeezes. The after party. Same world. Same run. Two eyes.
- **Enrich:** Reading one novela deepens the other. Her *click to see* is his *behind the doors*. Her *step in* is his *Ballerstown*. The housewife who presses the button and the baller who takes full advantage are **in the same story**—same campus, same consent, same vibe—from different sides.
- **Recursively:** Structure repeats at scale. Each novela can nest references to the other. Chapters can mirror (same scene, her chapter / his chapter). Future novelas in the line can do the same: twin POV, same story, recursive enrichment.
- **Nested:** One story contains the other. The Cage is nested in Full Advantage (she steps into his world). Full Advantage is nested in The Cage (he is the big boy in her book). The reader can enter from either door. The content is one continuum.
