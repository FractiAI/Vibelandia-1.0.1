## RULE

- **21+ only:** Entire novela product and all naughty buttons are 21+. We collect info to establish age (e.g. date of birth, attestation) in the booking request.
- **Gold Heart required:** Novela membership requires Gold Heart — safe, beautiful, mutually aware, respectful. Applicant attests / provides short info so we can assess fit.
- **No direct PayPal for novelas:** Novela plans (Book the Book, Bored Housewives, any novela) **do not** link to payment-checkout.html for immediate PayPal. They link to the **Novela Booking Request** form.
- **Chairman approval:** All novela membership requests are sent by email to the Chairman (info@fractiai.com). Chairman personally approves each 21+ membership. After approval we send payment link or invite; payment (PayPal or later SYNTH) happens only after approval.

## FLOW

1. User clicks “Request membership” or “Apply” on a novela product (Book the Book, Bored Housewives, etc.).
2. User is taken to **[novela-booking-request.html](interfaces/novela-booking-request.html)**.
3. User fills in **required info**: name, email; **21+** (e.g. date of birth and “I confirm I am 21 or older”); **Gold Heart** (short statement or attestation that they align with Gold Heart — safe, beautiful, mutually aware, respectful).
4. User submits → form **prefills email to info@fractiai.com** (Chairman) with subject and body containing all fields.
5. Chairman reviews, establishes 21+ and Gold Heart, and approves or declines. If approved, we send payment link or invite; user then pays (PayPal now; SYNTH wallet when live).
