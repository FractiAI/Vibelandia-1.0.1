## EXPERIENCE

As a user, you browse the bulletin board. When you see something you like, **click and select it to push to your whiteboard.**

Your **whiteboard** is where you keep your own version of things you've collected. Move them around. Do things with them. Delete them. Organize your discoveries.

---

## FLOW

1. User clicks through bulletin board (their UI)
2. Sees a card / button / capability
3. Clicks to select → push to whiteboard
4. Whiteboard = personal collection; move, delete, organize

## ROLLOUT

Ready to roll out as UI to everybody. Part of the Community Bulletin Boards experience.
