## 8. CANONICAL DISCLAIMER BLOCK (ADD WHERE APPROPRIATE)

Add to **legal footer** or **About / Credits** (e.g. index, payment-checkout, novela README, novel end matter):

**Names & trademarks:**  
*Our stories and brands use some real names and product names for atmosphere and comparison only. Historical figures (e.g. Chief Seattle, Mark Twain) are used in a fictional spirit; no endorsement by tribes, estates, or rightsholders. Character names inspired by real artists or entertainers (e.g. in the tradition of Frida Kahlo, classic entertainers, escape artists) are our original characters; no affiliation or endorsement. Stylistic references (e.g. Rick Rubin–style) are for vibe only; no affiliation or endorsement. Product and platform names (e.g. OnlyFans™, Disney™) are used for comparison only; all trademarks are property of their owners. We are not affiliated with the NFL or any team. Fully legal and respectful.*
