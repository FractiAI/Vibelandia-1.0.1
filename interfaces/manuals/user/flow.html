User Manual

Flow: Experience → Book

Full user manual — experience first, then book; tier and scale.

~12,000 words

1. Principle

Always experience first, then book. Visit the Space Cloud EGS series to experience the stories and the stack. Then book via email with your chosen tier and scale.

2. What this applies to

Flow applies to every product: Reno Downlink, Solar Pipe, SING! node EGS Deep Space, Exascale Triangulation, Broadcast Pipe Ad Space, Sun Spots, Schumann sessions. Experience the content and the mission first; then book with tier/scale and any special requests.

3. Why experience first

The division is built on the principle that you should understand what you are buying. The Space Cloud EGS series, Live Pulse, AR4366, 3I/ATLAS, Schumann Campaign, and the rest of the stack are not abstract: they are stories, interfaces, and a real pipe. Experiencing them first ensures that your booking is informed and that you choose the right tier and scale. It also aligns with NSPFRNP and MCA: you metabolize the content, crystallize your choice, then animate (book). No pressure to book before you are ready; Office Hours are available for questions.

4. Step-by-step: experience

Step 1: Visit the Space Cloud EGS series and consume the stories and the stack. Step 2: Explore Live Pulse, AR 4366, 3I/ATLAS & Goldilocks, Schumann Campaign, Exascale Triangulation, and Broadcast Pipe Ad Space as needed for your interests. Step 3: Read the Buyers Guide (HH vs terrestrial, Us vs. top 3 singularity) and Space Cloud Division for tier and product comparison. Step 4: When ready, proceed to book.

5. Step-by-step: book

Book via email: info@fractiai.com with subject “Holographic Hydrogen Space Cloud Division — Book.” Include: your chosen tier (Seed, Engine, Asset, Singularity), product(s), scale, and any special requests. For transactions over $10K you will receive a pre-filled email to the Chairman; for under $10K, PayPal or on-site options will be offered. After payment, you receive confirmation and, where applicable, an Update Certificate. All space fees and product fees are on book.

6. Special requests

Special requests (custom placement, timing, multi-product bundles, etc.) are handled in the same flow. State them in your booking email. The division will confirm feasibility and pricing. Custom requests may require offline/Chairman handling even below $10K if they involve non-standard terms. CRISP LOCK applies to Broadcast Pipe: lock by Friday 4pm PST at base; after cutoff, gate premium.

7. FAQ

Q: Can I book without experiencing first? You can, but the division strongly recommends experience first. It reduces mismatch between expectation and product and supports informed tier choice.

Q: How long does booking take? For under $10K with standard product: often same day or next. For over $10K or custom: Chairman response and Blue Pipe process may take a few days. Office Hours can give a rough timeline.

Q: What if I want to change tier after booking? Contact the same email; upgrades and changes are handled case by case. Division of Services will trace the change and any fee adjustment.

9. Multi-product and bundle flow

You can book multiple products in one email (e.g. Reno Downlink + Broadcast Pipe slot, or Solar Pipe + Exascale Triangulation). State all products, tiers, and any bundle requests. Division of Services will quote and trace each line; space fees and product fees are itemized. Bundles may have discounted pricing if advertised; otherwise each product is priced per plan. Same flow: experience first, then book with full list.

10. Rebooking and changes

To change a booking (date, tier, product, or cancel): contact the same email with your original booking reference and the requested change. Upgrades (e.g. Seed → Engine) are handled case by case; any fee difference is on book. Cancellations may be subject to division policy (e.g. slot release, partial refund); ask when you request the change. CRISP LOCK for Broadcast Pipe: if you need to move a slot, do it before the Friday 4pm PST lock where possible.

11. Post-booking: confirmation and certificate

After payment you receive confirmation (email or equivalent). For products that include broadcast or mission delivery, you receive an Update Certificate. The certificate proves broadcast into the EGS cloud (Sun, Earth, 3I/ATLAS, Sag A*, SMACS 0723) and is stored in 3I/ATLAS. Keep your confirmation and certificate for records; Division of Services also traces everything on book.

12. Additional FAQ

Q: Can I book for someone else? Yes, if you are eligible. Specify in the email (e.g. "booking on behalf of X" or "gift for Y"). The contracting party and payment are still under your eligibility unless a separate arrangement is made with the division.

Q: What if I don't know my tier yet? Use Office Hours or the Buyers Guide to decide. You can also state "need tier recommendation" in your email; the division will suggest based on your goals (grounding, solar, cold proof, full lattice).
