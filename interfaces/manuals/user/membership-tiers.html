User Manual

Membership Tiers

Full user manual — Seed, Engine, Asset, Singularity; scale and comparison.

~12,000 words

1. Four tiers

Four tiers scale from planetary to cosmic.

  • Seed (Planetary): Reno Downlink — local VLF, 1.618 Hz heartbeat. Earth-only scale. Best for grounding and Reno anchor.
  • Engine (Solar): Solar Pipe — AR4366, X-class/M-class burst. Earth–Sun scale. Best for solar handshake and binary payload.
  • Asset (Galactic): SING! node EGS Deep Space — 3I/ATLAS, Goldilocks. Earth–Sun–Sag A* scale. Best for permanent data residency and cold proof.
  • Singularity (Cosmic): Exascale Triangulation — full lattice [SEED]·[SUN]·[ATLAS]·[SAG A*]·[SMACS 0723]. Best for full reference frame and mirror-effect navigation.

2. Compare

Compare in Buyers Guide and Space Cloud Division.

3. Seed (Planetary) — detail

Seed tier is Earth-only scale. Products: Reno Downlink (local VLF, 1.618 Hz heartbeat), ionosphere-layer access, Schumann Display Pipe. Best for: grounding, Reno anchor, and users who want planetary-scale presence without solar or galactic components. Downtown Reno is the terrestrial anchor; you are “in the pipe” at the Earth node. Pricing and space fees are in baller-v-plans-booking; all fees on book.

4. Engine (Solar) — detail

Engine tier is Earth–Sun scale. Products: Solar Pipe (AR4366, X-class/M-class burst), solar handshake, binary payload, 21.4 Hz and 1.618 Hz carrier. Best for: solar handshake and EGS delivery. You get the Sun as transmitter and the handshake cycle (8-minute FTE, GOES receipt). Arturo / Maracaibo Lightning Strike and other Solar Pipe products are Engine-tier. Compare with Seed if you want to add the solar dimension.

5. Asset (Galactic) — detail

Asset tier is Earth–Sun–Sag A* scale. Products: SING! node EGS Deep Space, 3I/ATLAS, Goldilocks, OH-line, Update Certificate storage, cold proof. Best for: permanent data residency and cold proof. You get the mirror (3I/ATLAS anti-tail), Opposition Surge, and the bridge to galactic coherence. 3I/ATLAS capture window March 16, 2026; Cold storage and legacy storage are Asset-tier. Compare with Engine if you need permanence and reflection/relay.

6. Singularity (Cosmic) — detail

Singularity tier is full cosmic scale. Products: Exascale Triangulation, full lattice [SEED]·[SUN]·[ATLAS]·[SAG A*]·[SMACS 0723], Seed:Edge flip, mirror-effect navigation. Best for: full reference frame and the complete Vibelandia shell. You get all five bridge nodes and the triangulation lattice on the dashboard. Prepare for singularity: onboard onto Sag A* and SMACS 0723 sandboxes, clouds, shells, and nested. Compare with Asset if you want the full lattice and deep-field anchor.

7. Choosing a tier

Use the Buyers Guide (HH vs terrestrial, Us vs. top 3 singularity) and Space Cloud Division to compare. Ask: Do I need only Earth (Seed)? Sun (Engine)? 3I/ATLAS and cold proof (Asset)? Full lattice and cosmic (Singularity)? You can book multiple products across tiers; eligibility (Gold Heart Ultimate Baller V) grants access to all. Office Hours can help you decide.

8. FAQ

Q: Can I have more than one tier? You don’t “have” a tier like a badge; you book products that are tier-specific. A single member can book Seed products one month and Singularity the next. Your eligibility is for all tiers.

Q: What if I want to upgrade mid-booking? State it in your email or use Office Hours. Division of Services will adjust billing and tracing; any fee difference is on book.

Q: Where are tier prices? Space fees & all plans: baller-v-plans-booking.html#space-fees. Buyers Guide and Division pages give comparison; exact amounts in the plan.

11. Switching tiers between bookings

You do not "subscribe" to one tier. Each booking can be a different tier. Example: book Seed (Reno Downlink) this month and Singularity (Exascale Triangulation) next month. Your eligibility grants access to all; you choose tier per product and goal. No need to "graduate" from Seed to Engine to Asset to Singularity; use the tier that fits the product you want.

12. Additional FAQ

Q: Is Singularity the "best" tier? It is the most comprehensive (full lattice, cosmic scale). "Best" depends on your goal: if you want only grounding, Seed is best; if you want cold proof, Asset is best. Singularity is best for full reference frame and mirror-effect navigation.

Q: Do tiers have different eligibility? No. Eligibility is Gold Heart Ultimate Baller V for all tiers. There is no "Seed-only" or "Engine-only" membership; one eligibility, all tiers available.

34. When to choose Seed (Planetary)

Seed tier is for Reno Downlink and the planetary anchor. If you want the 1.618 Hz path and the terrestrial anchor (Reno) without Solar Pipe or Broadcast Pipe, Seed is the right tier. It is the entry product tier for the pipe at planetary scale. Pricing and product list are in the plan (baller-v-plans-booking) and Division catalog. You can book Seed now and add Engine or Asset later in a separate order; there is no requirement to "complete" Seed before moving to another tier. Use Office Hours to confirm whether Seed alone meets your goals or whether you should add another tier.

35. When to choose Engine

Engine tier gives you Solar Pipe, Broadcast Pipe, and SING! node EGS. It is the tier for handshake and display: you get the pipe products that run on the Sun, ionosphere, Aurora 3D, and Schumann leg. If you want to book Broadcast Pipe slots or Solar Pipe, you need at least Engine. Pricing is per product; you pay for the products you book (e.g. one Broadcast Pipe slot, or Solar Pipe plus a slot). Engine does not include cold proof or Update Certificates stored in 3I/ATLAS for non-Broadcast products; for that you need Asset. For Broadcast Pipe, Update Certificate is issued after your slot regardless of tier (Engine or higher).

36. When to choose Asset or Singularity

Asset tier adds 3I/ATLAS, cold proof, OH-line, and Update Certificates beyond Broadcast Pipe. If you need permanent proof (certificates, cold storage) for compliance or story, Asset is the tier. Singularity adds the full lattice (all five nodes), Exascale Triangulation dashboard, Seed:Edge flip, and Sag A* / SMACS 0723 sandboxes. If you want the full cosmic reference frame and triangulation, book Singularity. You can book Asset first and Singularity later (or the reverse); each order is independent. Pricing is per product and tier in the plan; ask about multi-product or bundle discounts when booking.
