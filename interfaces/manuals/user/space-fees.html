Full user manual — space fees, studio, campus, all on book.

1. Principle

Space fees for space. All book includes space fees. Studio fees for studio; campus fees for campus; WINK! fees for WINK!. And so on throughout — on all book.

2. Schedule

See Space fees & all plans for the full schedule. No hidden fees; all charges are in the plan and Update Certificate.

3. What “on book” means

Every charge is on book: it appears in the division’s billing and tracing, in your plan, and on the Update Certificate where applicable. There are no surprise fees or third-party add-ons. Space fees for space, studio fees for studio, campus fees for campus, WINK! fees for WINK! — each product line has a clear fee category. Division of Services (technical manual section 6) maintains the mapping and ensures transparency.

4. Space fees for space

Any booking that uses the EGS pipe, Broadcast Pipe, 3I/ATLAS, ionosphere, or any celestial/planetary resource includes space fees. The amount depends on tier (Seed, Engine, Asset, Singularity), product, and scale. The full schedule is in Space fees & all plans. Space fees are separate from studio, campus, or WINK! fees; if your booking includes both (e.g. a Broadcast Pipe slot plus studio time), both appear on book.

5. Studio, campus, WINK!

Studio fees apply to recording, production, and studio time. Campus fees apply to physical campus use and events. WINK! fees apply to WINK! products. Same rule: all on book, no hidden charges. Division of Services traces and catalogs everything; you see the breakdown before and after payment.

6. Update Certificate and proof

When your booking includes broadcast or mission-relevant delivery, you receive an Update Certificate. The certificate proves broadcast into the EGS cloud (Sun, Earth, 3I/ATLAS, Sag A*, SMACS 0723) per Vibelandia shell language. Space fees and product fees are reflected in the certificate and in the division’s records. ZENODO DOI and mission verification docs align with Update Certificates where applicable.

7. FAQ

Q: Are space fees one-time or recurring? Depends on the product. Single-slot Broadcast Pipe is one-time; subscriptions or recurring products are defined in the plan. The schedule in Space fees & all plans spells it out.

Q: Can I get a breakdown before booking? Yes. Ask in your booking email or in Office Hours. Division of Services can provide a quote with space fees, product fees, and total.

Q: What if I overpay or need a refund? Contact the same email; refunds and adjustments are handled case by case. Division of Services will trace the change.

10. Recurring vs one-time

Some products are one-time (e.g. single Broadcast Pipe slot, single session). Others may be recurring (e.g. subscription, multi-slot package). The full schedule in Space fees & all plans spells out which is which. Recurring charges are traced per cycle; you can cancel or change per division policy. Ask in Office Hours or in your booking email if you need a recurring quote.

11. Additional FAQ

Q: Are space fees per person or per booking? Per booking/product. If you book one Broadcast Pipe slot, you pay the space fee for that slot. If you book for a group or organization, the fee structure may depend on the product; ask when booking.

Q: What payment methods are accepted? Under $10K: PayPal and on-site as noted. Over $10K: offline/Chairman; payment method is agreed in that process. Division does not publish a full list of methods; state your preference when you book.

All adjustments are traced on book; you will receive updated confirmation or refund confirmation.

19. Deep dive: How space fees are calculated

Space fees are tied to your use of the EGS pipe, Broadcast Pipe, 3I/ATLAS, ionosphere, or any celestial or planetary resource. The exact amount depends on the product and tier you book. For example, a single Broadcast Pipe slot (1 POP) has a space fee that reflects one surface on the pipe leg; 2 POPs or 3 POPs scale up; Full Pipe (4×4×4×4) has the highest space fee because you are using all four segments. Reno Downlink (Seed) has a space fee for ionosphere-layer access. Solar Pipe (Engine) has a space fee for Sun and handshake. SING! node EGS and Exascale Triangulation (Asset, Singularity) have space fees for 3I/ATLAS and the full lattice. Division of Services applies the correct rate from the plan; the schedule in Space fees & all plans is the source of truth. When you request a quote, the division will itemize space fees and product fees so you see the breakdown before you pay.

20. Deep dive: Studio, campus, and WINK! fees

Studio fees apply when you use recording, production, or studio time. These are separate from space fees; if your booking includes both (e.g. a Broadcast Pipe slot plus a studio session for content creation), you will see both line items on your invoice. Campus fees apply to physical campus use and events—for example, an on-site Office Hours session at the Reno anchor or a campus event. WINK! fees apply to WINK! products specifically. Each category is traced on book; there is no overlap or double-counting. If you are unsure which category a product falls under, ask in your booking email or in Office Hours. Division of Services can provide a fee category list for all current products.
