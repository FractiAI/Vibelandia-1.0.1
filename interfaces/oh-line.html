What is the OH Line?

The OH line is the 3I/Chief Seattle OH line — not Office Hours. It is the ticker/feed channel for the 3I/Chief Seattle mission, part of 3I/ATLAS-LINK. Gap=0, Peak=1 binary gating. HELLO and RENO_ANCHOR packaged into OH-modulated format. When the Solar Pipe is hot, data flows into ATLAS Anti-Tail and sprays from Mini-Jets (120° Rotating Beacon).
