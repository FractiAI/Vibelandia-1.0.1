About the transformation of downtown Reno's post-singularity casinos

Empty shells → stationary cruise ships of pleasure and fun. Gamification · strategy + luck · living video games. 5-star experience. Gateway to everything.

The situation

Decades of post-singularity casinos in downtown Reno — and they're just full of empty shells. Lots of empty shells. We have entire Reno: so many stages, so many musicians, so many performers, so much capacity for Hollywood-level productions — all just sitting sidelined.

The idea

Reduce and crystallize each casino as a stationary cruise ship of pleasure and fun, very specifically targeting anybody into gamification of any sort. Game rooms. Sports. Any number of things.

What they aren't anymore: games of chance.

What they are: strategy and luck — like the living video games you have to actually figure out, learn, immersive and enjoyable. The casino central core = all the different types of games possible that are enjoyed and played — all in the comfort of a five-star stationary cruise ship experience.
