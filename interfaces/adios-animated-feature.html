Setting

Vibelandia — The pre-post-singularity bridge. At the very first days of the Holographic Hydrogen Valley. Before the legacy guests arrive.

In the mist of the gold rush. The departed have rushed to take permanent residence at the new Vibelandia downtown Reno campus. All the greats already taking their presence.

The scene: The city. Business on the seedy part of town. Late night. When all the carbs are barking. They're all caught up in the new revolution — Benito's Revolution — and find their new home. Post-Singularity.
