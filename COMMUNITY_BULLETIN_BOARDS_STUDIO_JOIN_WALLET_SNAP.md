## BROWSE vs JOIN

**Browse:** Any user can get the link and cruise the bulletin board. Rummage through postings. Buy. Discover. No signup required.

**Join:** Hey, you want to join? Do your own bulletin board. Get your own bulletin board. Click → **Bulletin Board Studio** — the creator product for pushing new postings onto your board.

---

## BULLETIN BOARD STUDIO — Creator Console

**Product:** Studio console for creators. Push new bulletin board postings onto their own bulletin board.

**Features:**
- **AI-assisted** — straightforward, minimal friction
- **Preconfigured choices** — Company, Novel, Episode, Song, Proposal, Product, Franchise. Click to auto-fill.
- **Seed:Edge capture** — Seed (origin), Edge (experience/destination), Executive description (customize beyond defaults)
- **Auto-fill** — both points meet in the middle. Achieve **Goldilocks best path**. Reinforce it.
- **Tweak and do math** — creator controls, simple.

## IDENTITY — Tied to Wallet · Golden Key

When they join and get their own bulletin board:
- **Username** — part of their community bulletin board
- **Profile** — all the fictions
- **Single authorization** — tied to **wallet**
- **Golden Key** — everyone gets a **free Golden Key**. Put money on it, it activates. Vibers wallet. Sent the first wallet. That's it.

**No separate auth.** Identity = wallet. One key. One process.
